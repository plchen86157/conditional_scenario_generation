Overview

We introduce a controllable collision scenario generation framework. Given an initial scenario, user-specified collision type and time-to-accident (TTA), our model predicts a Collision Pattern (the relative configuration between ego and attacker at impact), which then guides a trajectory planner to realize a feasible attacker motion.
